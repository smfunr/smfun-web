‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions
What is 1 "part" or "share"?
1 part = 0.1 ETH. It represents ownership in the sm.fun platform. Owners receive 35% of all platform fees.
How do I earn profits?
Profits come from: 1) 35% platform fee sharing, 2) Trading profits, 3) Referral bonuses, 4) Monthly distributions.
When can I withdraw profits?
Profits are distributed monthly. You can withdraw anytime from your Personal Center. Minimum withdrawal: 0.01 ETH.
Is there a minimum investment?
Minimum: 1 share (0.1 ETH). No maximum limit. We recommend starting with 10 shares (1 ETH) for Silver membership benefits.
What wallets are supported?
MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, Phantom, Trust Wallet, and any EIP-1193 compatible wallet.
How secure is my investment?
We use industry-standard security: wallet authentication, encrypted data, regular audits, and transparent blockchain records.
